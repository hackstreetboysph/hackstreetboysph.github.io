About Us

We are a team of certified professionals with huge interest in various areas of cybersecurity, as well as playing Capture the Flag.

We aim to be an organization that encourages knowledge-sharing — a place where all the members of our team can gain direct mentorship from each other through join discussions and continuous participation.

We also aim to be a close-knit team that may, (hopefully) one day, regularly put the Philippines in the international CTF world map.

We hope that through this team, we may be able to share our knowledge and expertise to fellow countrymen — especially the younger generations — so that someday, they may carry the same ambition and goals that we had when we formed this team.
